At Shikamana School the academic curriculum is central to our philosophy of an excellent education. We also believe however, that to be a truly all-round school which offers children opportunities to realise potential, many valuable experiences are to be found outside of the normal academic curriculum. Collectively, these experiences contribute to the development of the whole person, and help to cement together the Shikamana experience, defining what is special about us. It is in an environment of supportive teachers and parents where children feel safe to learn, and happily face new and challenging opportunities.
